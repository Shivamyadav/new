Our Vision

"Coming up with solutions to the problems that question our sustenance"

QuestFEW is not merely a competition, but a journey of development in itself that aims to bring about a revolution in the sustainability space by building an outstanding community of like-minded individuals working towards a common goal. We believe that the collaborative efforts of a dedicated community, combined with effective ideation and innovation are powerful enough to challenge the status quo.

Understanding the Quest

The Quest for FOOD, ENERGY, and WATER

QuestFEW is more than a National level competition; it’s a journey of development that calls for interested students to develop out-of-the-box solutions to deal with the ever-increasing stress on the world’s food, energy and water resources.

Your interest and passion are enough to get you started with this wonderful journey of collaboration and learning. And with the Getting Started resources at your disposal.

Food

Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger.

Feeding the world population satisfactorily is a milestone that we are struggling to achieve in the present. By the next three decades, the world population is expected to reach 9 billion and feeding them will be a quest for humanity’s collective future.

Energy

We’re using 50% more electricity today then we were using a decade ago.

With the exponential increase in urbanization and industrialization, the eminent stress on our energy resources is pushing the mankind to come up with alternative energy solution.

Water

Over 1.5 billion do not have access to clean, safe water.

There is a quintessential need to address the global crisis caused by insufficient water supply to satisfy the growing demands on the world’s water resources to meet human, commercial and agricultural needs. 

Why questFEW

"The mega event- The National Finals Summit? The amazing prizes? Or the Learning Curve?"

QuestFEW is your chance to get started on a wonderful journey of learning and contribution, and on this journey, you’ll have an opportunity to be coached by wonderful mentors who’ll guide and support your action plans to success, and will immensely contribute to helping you develop Leadership and Business skills needed to ace this competition.

Not only this, if you get selected as a finalist, you’ll receive a sponsored invitation to the National Finals event of questFEW 2016, scheduled in the capital of India, New Delhi. The Finals event will see some pioneer speakers and industry professionals from the sustainability space, a networking event, followed by the project pitching round, at the end of which the winners will be awarded amazing prizes. If you are passionate enough to make it happen, you will!
