Satinderpal Singh

Satinderpal is a speaker, a problem solver and a case-study enthusiast. He is passionate about playing an instrumental role in the sustainability space in order to contribute towards a better future for mankind by addressing some major global issues.
QuestFEW is a National level initiative to raise the issue of sustainability, and acts as a platform to connect passionate students from all over India to come together and develop outstanding solutions in the sustainability space in order to tackle the issue of food insecurity, water shortage, and energy crisis. In the past, Satinder has been a part of numerous remarkable involvements relating to the topic of sustainable development, and some of the milestones that he has achieved so far in his quest to make a mark are: Working on a Solar Car project for the American Solar Challenge to uphold the theme of renewable energy applications; establishing an E-waste management enterprise in New Delhi with a sustainable business model to deal with the environmental hazards of e-waste; developing a solution to tackle the issue of food insecurity by suggesting an alternative solution to meet the growing demands of livestock feed. Also, he’s been selected for International programmes in the past like the Asia Pacific Youth Exchange (Thailand), and International Youth to Youth Summit (Dubai).

- Satinderpal Singh '17



About QuestFEW

“The prime motivation for me behind setting up QuestFEW was to form an amazing community of changemakers that are passionate about addressing some major global challenges that question the very essence of our future generations, and willing to take up the task of developing real-world solutions for the same. I strongly believe that the collective efforts of young and motivated individuals can contribute substantially to develop solutions to such issues; and it is the earnest endeavors like these that define the very movements capable of bringing about a revolution of breakthrough changes."
